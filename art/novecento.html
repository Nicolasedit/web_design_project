Museo Novecento
The Museo del Novecento ("museum of the twentieth century") is a museum of twentieth-century art in Milan.
It is housed in the Palazzo dell'Arengario, near Piazza del Duomo in the centre of the city. The museum opened in December 2010. It displays about 400 works, most of them Italian, from the twentieth century.
Apart from a single room housing works by foreign artists including Braque, Kandinsky, Klee, Léger, Matisse, Mondrian and Picasso, the majority of the works exhibited in the museum are by Italian artists.
A major section is devoted to the Italian Futurists, with works by Giacomo Balla, Umberto Boccioni and Carlo Carrà.
